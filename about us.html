We Give What Users Need

Guiding you

They’re people like you,
going your way

What makes the Xplore experience truly great are the people behind the wheel. They are mothers and fathers. Students and teachers. Veterans. Neighbors. Friends. Our partners drive their own cars—on their own schedule—in cities big and small. Which is why more than one million people worldwide have signed up to guide.

Helping cities

For the good of all.

A city with Xplore has more economic opportunities for residents, fewer drunk drivers on the streets, and better access to transportation for those without it.

Safety

Putting people first.

Whether riding in the backseat or driving up front, every part of the Xplore experience has been designed around your safety and security.
